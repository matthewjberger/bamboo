# E-commerce Website

A fully custom e-commerce platform built for Artisan Goods Co, a marketplace for handcrafted products from independent makers.

## Challenge

Artisan Goods needed more than a standard Shopify store. They wanted a unique shopping experience that told the story of each maker and their craft.

## Technical Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js with TypeScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **CMS**: Sanity.io for content management
- **Commerce**: Stripe for payments
- **Hosting**: Vercel with edge functions

## Features

### Maker Profiles
Each artisan has a dedicated profile page showcasing their story, process, and products.

### Product Stories
Products include rich media - videos of the creation process, material sourcing information, and care instructions.

### Custom Checkout
A streamlined checkout experience optimized for mobile, with Apple Pay and Google Pay support.

### Admin Dashboard
A custom admin panel for managing orders, inventory, and maker payouts.

## Results

- 40% increase in average order value
- 25% reduction in cart abandonment
- Featured in "Best E-commerce Design 2023"
- 98% uptime with zero downtime during peak sales
